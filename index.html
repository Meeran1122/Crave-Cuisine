Menu

Discover our mouthwatering selection of dishes,
crafted with fresh ingredients and bursting with flavor. Dive into a world of taste
and let every bite tell a story!



Biryani

A flavorful rice dish with aromatic spices, tender
chicken, and basmati rice, often served with raita.

Chicken Karahi

A rich, spicy chicken curry cooked in a wok-like
pan with tomatoes, green chilies, and fresh herbs.

Nihari

A slow-cooked beef stew, seasoned with a blend
of spices, served with naan or rice.

Haleem

A savory stew made from wheat, barley, lentils,
and meat, cooked to perfection with spices.

Seekh Kebabs

Minced meat skewers seasoned with spices and
grilled to perfection.

Aloo Gosht

A curry made with tender meat (usually mutton or beef)
and potatoes, cooked in a rich, flavorful gravy.

Chapli Kebab

A traditional minced meat kebab, spiced and
fried, often served with naan or rice.

Daal Chawal

A simple yet hearty dish of lentils cooked with spices, served
with steamed rice. It's a staple comfort food in Pakistani households.

Shahi Tukda

A rich dessert made from fried bread soaked in sweet
milk, garnished with nuts and cardamom.

Peshawari Chapli Kebab

A variant of chapli kebabs from Peshawar, made with
minced meat, herbs, and spices, grilled to perfection.
